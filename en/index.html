"IT'S JUST A CIGARETTE"

PHOTOSHOP MASHUP

Mashup realized during my professional degree in Multimedia, audiovisual and graphic design.

My inspiration was the artist Paul Fuentes, mainly for those pastel color, I added my personal touch with the noise and the vignette, to ‘break’ this flat aspect, and to give a vintage look.

The message in the mashup is clear, report the danger of the cigarette, to much present in France.


Inspirations :

• Titre : Princess Chelsea - The Cigarette Duet

"FISHEYE"

PHOTOMONTAGE

This visual was made during DUT (Technical and university degree). The aim was to take a portrait of one of the two people in the group, and based on an existing work, create a poster denouncing society problem.

I realized the photomontage on Photoshop, while my partner served as a model. We have chosen to denounce overfishing, a too much forgotten cause. Our inspiration for this project is the artist Flora Borsi, specialized in self-portraits, who has taken a series of photos with this eye on eye superposition with animals.


Credits and inspirations:

• Photo credits: Nicolas Brante
• Inspiration : Flora Borsi

TYPOGRAPHY SPECIMEN

A type font made during my professional degree in Corte.

Created on the website : Prototypo according to instructions given on TypeCooker.


Instructions :

• Weight : Semi Bold
• Ascender : Longer than normal
• Stems : Straight
• Contrast Amount : Visible
• Width : Normal
• Stroke ending : No serif
• Descender : Shorter than normal
